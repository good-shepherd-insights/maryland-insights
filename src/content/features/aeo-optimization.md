### What Is AEO — and Why Does It Matter for Maryland Small Businesses?
Search has changed. When a homeowner in Rockville asks Google "who's the best plumber near me," or a shopper in Annapolis says "find me a local gift shop open today," the answer they get often comes from AI-powered results — not the traditional blue links.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content so AI tools, voice assistants, and Google's AI Overviews choose your business as the answer. For Maryland small businesses competing against larger brands, AEO is one of the fastest ways to level the playing field and get found first.

### How Maryland Customers Are Searching Right Now
Your customers aren't just typing keywords anymore. They're asking full questions — and expecting direct answers. Here's what that looks like in practice:

* "What's the best HVAC company in Baltimore County?"
* "Is there a local accountant near Annapolis that handles small businesses?"
* "Which Frederick restaurants are open for lunch on Sundays?"
* "Find a Maryland contractor who does bathroom remodels under $10,000."

If your website isn't structured to answer these questions directly, AI search results will skip you — even if you've been in business for years.
