### Senior Frontend Developer

We are seeking a passionate and experienced **Senior Frontend Developer** to take a leading role in developing the user interface of our innovative AI-driven platform. You will work with a talented team of engineers and designers to build a product that is both powerful and a joy to use.

**Gross annual compensation range:** $120,000–$150,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a seasoned frontend developer with a strong sense of product ownership and a passion for crafting exceptional user experiences. You are comfortable working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment and are always looking for ways to improve your craft.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 5+ years of professional experience with modern JavaScript frameworks (React, Vue, or Svelte)
- Deep understanding of HTML, CSS, and web performance best practices
- Experience with TypeScript and its application in large-scale projects
- A strong portfolio of projects that demonstrates your skills and creativity
- Excellent problem-solving abilities and a keen eye for detail
- Strong communication skills and the ability to mentor other engineers

---

##### Responsibilities

- Lead the development of new user-facing features from conception to launch
- Build reusable components and frontend libraries for future use
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability
- Collaborate with designers to translate UI/UX designs into high-quality code
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to a culture of high-quality engineering

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a $2,500 home office setup budget
- $2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
