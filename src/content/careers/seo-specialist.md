### SEO Specialist

We are looking for an **SEO Specialist** to help us improve our search engine rankings. You will be responsible for conducting keyword research, optimizing our website for search engines, and building high-quality backlinks. You will work closely with our marketing and content teams to create a cohesive and effective SEO strategy.

**Gross annual compensation range:** $80,000–$100,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a data-driven and analytical SEO specialist with a passion for search engines. You are a strong communicator and collaborator who is able to work effectively in a team. You are a creative problem-solver who is always looking for ways to improve our search engine rankings.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 3+ years of experience as an SEO specialist
- A strong understanding of SEO principles and best practices
- Experience with SEO tools such as Google Analytics, Google Search Console, and Ahrefs
- Experience with keyword research and on-page optimization
- Experience with link building
- Excellent analytical and communication skills

---

##### Responsibilities

- Conduct keyword research to identify opportunities for growth
- Optimize our website for search engines
- Build high-quality backlinks to our website
- Monitor our search engine rankings and traffic
- Stay up-to-date on the latest trends in SEO
