### Product Marketing Manager

We are looking for a **Product Marketing Manager** to own the positioning, messaging, and go-to-market strategy for our products. You will be the bridge between our product, marketing, and sales teams, ensuring that we are effectively communicating the value of our platform to the right audiences.

**Gross annual compensation range:** $110,000–$140,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a natural storyteller with a deep understanding of the developer market. You are a strategic thinker who can translate complex technical features into compelling benefits and value propositions. You are data-driven and have a track record of successful product launches.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 4+ years of experience in product marketing, preferably in the B2B SaaS or developer tools space
- Proven ability to develop and execute successful go-to-market strategies
- Excellent writing and communication skills, with a portfolio of marketing content
- Strong analytical skills and experience with marketing analytics tools
- Ability to work cross-functionally and manage multiple projects simultaneously
- A passion for technology and the developer community

---

##### Responsibilities

- Develop product positioning and messaging that differentiates our products in the market
- Plan and execute go-to-market strategies for new features and products
- Create compelling marketing content, including website copy, blog posts, case studies, and sales collateral
- Conduct market and competitor research to inform our marketing strategy
- Work closely with the product team to understand the product roadmap and identify marketing opportunities
- Enable the sales team with the training and materials they need to be successful

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a $2,500 home office setup budget
- $2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
