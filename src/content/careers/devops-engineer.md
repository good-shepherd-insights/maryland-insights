### DevOps Engineer

We are looking for a **DevOps Engineer** to help us build and maintain our cloud infrastructure. You will work closely with our development teams to ensure that our applications are delivered and deployed in a secure, reliable, and efficient manner.

**Gross annual compensation range:** $120,000–$160,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are an experienced DevOps engineer with a passion for automation and a deep understanding of cloud technologies. You are a proactive problem-solver who is always looking for ways to improve efficiency and reliability.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 5+ years of experience in a DevOps or SRE role
- Strong experience with cloud platforms like AWS, Google Cloud, or Azure
- Experience with infrastructure as code (e.g., Terraform, CloudFormation)
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions)
- Experience with containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes)
- Strong scripting skills (e.g., Python, Bash)

---

##### Responsibilities

- Design, build, and maintain our cloud infrastructure
- Automate our build, test, and deployment processes
- Monitor our systems and applications for performance and availability
- Troubleshoot and resolve infrastructure issues
- Work with development teams to ensure that our applications are designed for scalability and reliability
- Implement and maintain security best practices

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a $2,500 home office setup budget
- $2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
