### UX Designer

We are looking for a creative and analytical **UX Designer** to join our design team. You will be responsible for creating user-centered designs for our products, from concept to execution. You will work closely with our product, engineering, and marketing teams to create a seamless and intuitive user experience.

**Gross annual compensation range:** €60,000–€70,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a passionate and experienced UX designer with a strong portfolio of user-centered designs. You are a creative problem-solver who is always looking for ways to improve the user experience. You are a strong communicator and collaborator who is able to work effectively in a team.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 3+ years of experience as a UX designer
- A strong portfolio of user-centered designs
- Experience with design tools such as Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD
- Experience with user research and usability testing
- A strong understanding of user-centered design principles
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

---

##### Responsibilities

- Create user-centered designs for our products, from concept to execution
- Work closely with our product, engineering, and marketing teams to create a seamless and intuitive user experience
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform your designs
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
- Advocate for the user and ensure that their needs are met
- Contribute to our design system and help to maintain design consistency across our products

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a €2,000 home office setup budget
- €2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
