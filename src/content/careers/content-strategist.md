### Content Strategist

We are looking for a **Content Strategist** to help us create and manage our content strategy. You will be responsible for planning, creating, and distributing content that is valuable, relevant, and consistent. You will work closely with our marketing, product, and design teams to create a cohesive and effective content strategy.

**Gross annual compensation range:** $90,000–$110,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a strategic and creative content strategist with a passion for storytelling. You are a strong writer and editor with a deep understanding of content marketing. You are a strong communicator and collaborator who is able to work effectively in a team.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 4+ years of experience as a content strategist or content marketer
- A strong portfolio of content that you have created
- Experience with content management systems (CMS)
- Experience with SEO and a deep understanding of how to create content that ranks well in search engines
- A strong understanding of content marketing principles
- Excellent writing, editing, and communication skills

---

##### Responsibilities

- Plan, create, and distribute content that is valuable, relevant, and consistent
- Work closely with our marketing, product, and design teams to create a cohesive and effective content strategy
- Manage our content calendar and ensure that we are publishing content on a regular basis
- Measure the effectiveness of our content and make data-driven decisions about our content strategy
- Stay up-to-date on the latest trends in content marketing and SEO

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a $2,500 home office setup budget
- $2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
