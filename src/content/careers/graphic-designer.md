### Graphic Designer

We are looking for a **Graphic Designer** to help us create beautiful and engaging visual content. You will be responsible for creating a variety of visual assets, including illustrations, icons, and marketing materials. You will work closely with our marketing and product teams to create a consistent and compelling visual brand.

**Gross annual compensation range:** €40,000–€50,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You are a creative and talented graphic designer with a strong portfolio of visual work. You are a strong communicator and collaborator who is able to work effectively in a team. You are a creative problem-solver who is always looking for ways to improve our visual brand.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 3+ years of experience as a graphic designer
- A strong portfolio of visual work
- Experience with design tools such as Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and InDesign
- Experience with creating a variety of visual assets, including illustrations, icons, and marketing materials
- A strong understanding of design principles
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills

---

##### Responsibilities

- Create a variety of visual assets, including illustrations, icons, and marketing materials
- Work closely with our marketing and product teams to create a consistent and compelling visual brand
- Help to maintain our design system and ensure that our visual brand is consistent across all of our products
- Advocate for our visual brand and ensure that it is represented in the best possible way

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a €2,000 home office setup budget
- €2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
