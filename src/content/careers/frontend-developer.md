### Frontend Developer

We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented **UI Designer** to join one of our dynamic, cross-functional product teams. You’ll play a key role in shaping the look, feel, and usability of our digital experiences, ensuring everything we build is not only functional but visually engaging and intuitive.

**Gross annual compensation range:** €58,000–€65,000 (depending on experience, with guaranteed annual salary reviews)

---

##### About Us

We’re a global team of 100+ passionate professionals working remotely from 40+ countries. We value flexibility and autonomy—you can work from your home office, a co-working space in Berlin, or a beachside café in Bali. A few times a year, we gather in inspiring locations around the world for meaningful team retreats.

---

##### About You

You’re an experienced **UI Designer** who’s passionate about creating user-centered designs that are both beautiful and purposeful. You thrive in a collaborative environment and enjoy turning complex problems into clean, accessible solutions.

**We’d love to hear from you if you have:**

- 3+ years of UI design experience for web or mobile applications
- Strong portfolio showcasing design systems, responsive design, and user interfaces
- Proficiency with Figma and other industry-standard design tools
- Basic understanding of UX principles and front-end technologies (HTML, CSS)
- Ability to work with design systems and contribute to their evolution
- Excellent communication skills and attention to detail

---

##### Responsibilities

- Design visually polished, user-friendly interfaces in collaboration with UX designers and developers
- Contribute to and maintain our evolving design system
- Create high-fidelity mockups, prototypes, and handoff-ready files
- Work closely with Product Managers and Engineers to ensure seamless implementation
- Advocate for users and design consistency across all platforms
- Participate in user research and usability testing efforts

---

##### Benefits

Time off isn’t just about rest—it’s an opportunity to reset, grow, and refocus. Here’s what we offer:

- 24 paid days off per year + your local public holidays
- Company-sponsored retreats (fully covered travel & lodging)
- Laptop and a €2,000 home office setup budget
- €2,000/year wellness & mental health allowance
- Co-working space or internet reimbursement
- Paid sabbaticals after 4 years of tenure
- Flexible schedule—design your workday around your life
