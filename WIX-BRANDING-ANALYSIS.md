RESOURCES
- Explore (Blog, AI features, Design inspiration)

### Navigation Philosophy

- **Solution-oriented** - Organized by user need, not product category
- **Progressive depth** - High-level categories, expandable sub-menus
- **Clear hierarchy** - Primary actions visible, secondary accessible
- **Cross-selling** - Always suggesting next steps ("hire a pro," "explore blog")

## Summary

Wix branding success comes from:

1. **Clarity** - Know who you are, who you are for, what you offer
2. **Consistency** - Every page, every element reinforces the same message
3. **Customer-first language** - Always frame from user perspective
4. **Low-friction entry** - Remove every possible barrier to getting started
5. **Risk reversal** - Guarantees, free trials, no credit card required
6. **Outcome focus** - Not features, but what features accomplish

The biggest lesson: **Wix does not try to be everything to everyone within their messaging.** They have clear segments with tailored pathways, but the core brand voice remains consistent.
