# Content Gap Analysis: Maryland Insights

## Analysis Scope
- **Site:** Maryland Insights (marylandinsights.com)
- **Competitors:** [National Builders (e.g., Wix, Squarespace)], [Local Maryland Agencies]
- **Topic Focus:** Website Builder, Local SEO, AEO Optimization, Analytics & Reporting, Marketing Automation, AI-Powered Content.
- **Audience:** Maryland Small Businesses
- **Business Goals:** Increase user acquisition for the Website Builder, boost leads for local SEO & marketing services, establish brand authority in Maryland.

## Executive Summary
Maryland Insights already excels at geo-targeted content and long-tail explanations of how features benefit Maryland businesses across various counties. However, we have significant gaps compared to national competitors on top-of-funnel educational topics (like "how to start a business in Maryland") and comparative formats ("Maryland Insights vs. Wix"). Compared to local agency competitors, we are missing in-depth case studies and transparent pricing/ROI calculators.

## Audit of Existing Content
- **Strengths:** Strong cluster of feature pages (`website-builder`, `local-seo`, `analytics-reporting`, etc.) with built-in AEO (FAQs) and localized keyword mentions (e.g., Montgomery County, Baltimore City).
- **Weaknesses:** Lacks middle-of-funnel comparison content. Needs more bottom-of-funnel case studies and templated guides.

## Keyword & Topic Gaps (Prioritized)

### High Priority (Quick Wins)
1. **"Best website builder for local business"** - National competitors dominate this. We need a targeted post outlining why a geo-focused builder wins over generic ones.
2. **"Maryland small business marketing agency"** - We are missing a pillar page directly comparing our software/service hybrid model against traditional local agencies.
3. **"[Competitor] alternative in Maryland"** - We need dedicated comparison pages (e.g., "Squarespace alternative for Maryland businesses").

### Strategic Builds (Medium Term)
1. **Pillar Page: "The Ultimate Guide to Local SEO in Maryland"** - We have a feature page, but competitors have 5,000-word educational guides.
2. **"How to start a business in Maryland"** - Top-of-funnel gap. Competitors use this to capture leads early in the business lifecycle.
3. **AI Search Engine Queries** - Missing explicit Q&A content around "Why isn't my business showing up on ChatGPT/Perplexity?" (Though we have one blog post on this, it needs to be expanded into a cluster).

### Long-term (Low Volume / High Intent)
1. **Industry-specific Maryland Guides** - "Website builder for Maryland Plumbers", "Local SEO for Baltimore Restaurants".

## Content Format Gaps
- **Comparison Pages:** None currently exist. Competitors use "Us vs. Them" extensively.
- **Case Studies / Success Stories:** Existing content mentions generic "real-world examples" but lacks deep, data-driven case studies of specific Maryland businesses.
- **Tools/Templates:** Missing free tools (e.g., "Maryland Local SEO Audit Tool" or "Maryland Business Name Generator").

## Action Plan
- **Month 1:** Develop the "Competitor Alternative" landing pages. Expand the AEO/FAQ sections on existing feature pages.
- **Month 2:** Write the "Ultimate Guide to Local SEO in Maryland" and create industry-specific sub-pages.
- **Month 3:** Build a template/tool to capture top-of-funnel leads (e.g., a free local listing scan).

## Success Metrics
- **Metric 1:** Increase organic traffic for "Maryland website builder" and related terms by 30% in 6 months.
- **Metric 2:** Achieve map pack visibility for "local SEO Maryland" queries.
- **Metric 3:** Increase conversion rate on comparison pages vs. standard feature pages.
