# BRANDING(maryland-insights-rebrand).md

## Objective

Create a comprehensive branding plan that adapts Wix/Squarespace's enterprise-level expert copywriting and positioning for Maryland Insights, using Maryland businesses as the unique selling proposition (USP).

---

## Phase 1: Brand Positioning & Core Messaging

### 1.1 Hero Section Rewrite

**Current:**
> "Maryland's Leading Website & Growth Partner"
> "Turn your website into a steady business. Maryland's #1 website builder for small businesses uses data-driven insights to deliver real revenue growth."

**Target (Wix/Squarespace style):**
> "The new way to build your Maryland business website"
> "Go from words to a business-ready site that's entirely yours to shape, built specifically for Maryland small businesses. Dominate local search, attract Maryland customers, and grow your business with AI-powered tools."

**Justification:** Wix uses "The new way to create a website" as their hook. We adapt it to "The new way to build your Maryland business website" - same structure, Maryland-focused. Squarespace uses "A website makes it real" - emotional hook. Our version: "Built specifically for Maryland small businesses" - geographic differentiation.

### 1.2 Tagline Unification

**Current variations to consolidate:**
- "Maryland's #1 website builder for small businesses"
- "Turn your website into a steady business"
- "AI-powered growth and SEO services that turn your site into a steady revenue engine"
- "Ready to Grow Your Maryland Business?"

**Target tagline:**
> "Build. Rank. Grow. — For Maryland Businesses"

**Justification:** Simple, memorable, three-word structure like Wix's "Create without limits" or Squarespace's "Built to stand out, ready to scale." The dash emphasizes it's specifically for Maryland businesses.

### 1.3 Friction Removal Messaging

**Current:**
- "No Credit Card"
- "Free Forever"
- "Cancel Anytime"

**Target (align with Wix/Squarespace):**
> "Start for free. No credit card required."
> "Free forever. Cancel anytime."

**Justification:** Wix says "Start for free. No credit card required." - this is the gold standard. We adapt exactly the same structure but with our "Free forever" messaging. Shorter, punchier, consistent.

## Phase 4: Feature Presentation

### 4.1 Services/Features Rewriting

**Current (confusing mix):**
| Service | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Website Builder | "Custom website design and development that converts visitors into customers." |
| SEO Optimization | "Search engine optimization and ranking strategies that drive organic traffic." |
| Local SEO | "Google Maps, local citations, and Maryland-focused ranking strategies." |
| Analytics & Reporting | "Track performance, analyze data, and make informed decisions with detailed reports." |
| Marketing Automation | "Automate campaigns, personalize messaging, and optimize your marketing workflows." |
| AI-Powered Content | "AI-generated content and copywriting tailored to your brand voice and audience." |

**Target (Wix/Squarespace style - benefit-driven):**

| Feature | Headline | Description |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Website Builder | "Build a professional Maryland website" | "Choose from professionally designed templates or let AI create your site. No coding required — your Maryland business online in minutes." |
| SEO Optimization | "Rank higher in Maryland search results" | "Dominate local Maryland search. Our proven SEO strategies help your business get found by Maryland customers searching for your services." |
| Local SEO | "Dominate Maryland local search" | "Google Maps optimization, Maryland citations, and local rankings that put your business on the map — literally." |
| Analytics | "Know your Maryland audience" | "Track who's visiting your site, where they come from, and what converts Maryland visitors into customers." |
| Marketing Automation | "Automate your Maryland marketing" | "Set it and forget it. Automated campaigns that nurture Maryland leads while you focus on your business." |
| AI Content | "AI content made for Maryland businesses" | "Generate compelling copy, product descriptions, and marketing content tailored to your Maryland audience — in seconds." |

**Justification:** Wix/Squarespace present features as solutions to problems, not technical capabilities. "Sell products" not "eCommerce platform." We do the same: "Rank higher in Maryland search" not "SEO Optimization."

### 4.2 Feature Categories
