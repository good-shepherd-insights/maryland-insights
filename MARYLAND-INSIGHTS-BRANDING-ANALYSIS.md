# Maryland Insights Branding Strategy Analysis

A comprehensive breakdown of the current Maryland Insights brand positioning, messaging, inconsistencies, and comparison to industry leaders Wix and Squarespace.

---

## Executive Summary

Maryland Insights is an Astro-based directory website that positions itself as a website builder and growth partner for Maryland small businesses. However, the current branding suffers from significant ambiguity, template remnants, and inconsistencies that undermine its credibility. This report analyzes every aspect of the current branding and provides comparison to industry standards.

### Pricing Analysis

**Critical Issues:**

1. **No annual discount** — Business and Business Pro are same price monthly/yearly. Industry standard is 20-50% discount for annual.

2. **$0 "Starter" is misleading** — "Free Forever" messaging in hero conflicts with service-based offerings (SEO, Local SEO don't make sense as self-serve)

3. **AI Credits are vague** — What does "$5 of AI credit" actually get you? How many generations? This is not clear.

4. **Pricing psychology missing** — No "most popular" badge, no recommended tier, no upgrade path explanation

5. **Enterprise is vague** — "Custom pricing" with no starting point loses price-sensitive customers

Maryland Insights has significant potential but currently suffers from:
